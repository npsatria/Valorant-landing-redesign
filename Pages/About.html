BEYOND THE GAME

THE VALORANT PROTOCOL

VALORANT is a free-to-play, 5v5 character-based tactical shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Set in the near-future Earth, it blends precise, highly lethal gunplay with the unique skills of a diverse cast of Agents.

A global shadow organization formed the VALORANT Protocol to address the existential threat following the mysterious event known as First Light. They recruited agents from all over the world to secure Radianite across the globe.

CRAFTED BY RIOT GAMES

Building on a decade of experience operating League of Legends, Riot Games set out to build a genre-defining tactical shooter. VALORANT is built on core tenets: highly consequential gunplay, strategic depth, and a commitment to anti-cheat and performant servers.

The game engine ensures competitive integrity, guaranteeing that if you click heads, your shots will land. Combined with a robust continuous update schedule, VALORANT is built to be a forever game.
