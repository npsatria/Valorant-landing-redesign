WE ARE VALORANT

A global stage where precision meets creativity. We are the architects of chaos and the masters of tactical play.

DEFY THE LIMITS

Blend your style and experience on a global, competitive stage. You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side using sharp gunplay and tactical abilities. With one life per round, you'll need to think faster than your opponent if you want to survive.

Take on foes across Competitive and Unranked modes as well as Deathmatch and Spike Rush. It's not just about the win—it's about how you get there.

DIVERSITY IN AGENTS

More than guns and bullets, you’ll choose an Agent armed with adaptive, swift, and lethal abilities that create opportunities to let your gunplay shine. No two Agents play alike, just as no two highlight reels will look the same.

From the shadows of Omen to the flames of Phoenix, every Agent is a different way to play the game you love.
