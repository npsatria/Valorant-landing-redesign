FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

VALORANT is a 5v5 character-based tactical shooter developed and published by Riot Games. Players take on the role of Agents, characters designed based on several countries and cultures around the world. In the main game mode, players are assigned to either the attacking or defending team.

Yes, VALORANT is entirely free to download and play. However, players can purchase an in-game currency called Valorant Points (VP) to buy cosmetic items such as weapon skins, player cards, and gun buddies. These items do not provide any gameplay advantages.

VALORANT is designed to run on a wide variety of PC hardware. Minimum requirements (30 FPS) include an Intel Core 2 Duo E8400 or AMD Athlon 200GE CPU, and an Intel HD 4000 or AMD Radeon R5 200 GPU. Recommended (60+ FPS) requires an Intel i3-4150 or AMD Ryzen 3 1200 CPU, and an NVIDIA GeForce GT 730 or AMD Radeon R7 240 GPU.

New players can unlock two Agents relatively quickly by completing the introductory "Welcome Contract." After that, Agents can be unlocked by playing the game and earning XP, managing Agent Recruitment events, or by purchasing them with Valorant Points (VP).
