SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

VALORANT is optimized to run on a wide variety of PC hardware. Minimum specs include Intel Core 2 Duo E8400 and Intel HD 4000 GPU. For 144+ FPS, we recommend Intel i5-9400F and GTX 1050 Ti.

IS VALORANT FREE TO PLAY?

Yes, VALORANT is 100% free to download and play. You can unlock Agents for free through gameplay, or purchase cosmetic weapon skins and a Battlepass with VALORANT Points.

WHAT IS VANGUARD?

Riot Vanguard is Riot Games' custom anti-cheat software, designed to maintain the highest level of competitive integrity in our games. It consists of a client and a kernel-mode driver.

HOW DO I UNLOCK NEW AGENTS?

New players can unlock their first two Agents through the "New Player Experience" contract. Afterward, you can unlock Agents by activating their specific recruitment events or using Kingdom Credits.
