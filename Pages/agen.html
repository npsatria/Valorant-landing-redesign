CHOOSE YOUR AGENT

Creativity is your greatest weapon. More than guns and bullets, you'll choose an Agent armed with adaptive, swift, and lethal abilities that create opportunities to let your gunplay shine.

GEKKO

MOSH PIT (C)

EQUIP Mosh. FIRE to throw Mosh like a grenade. Mosh duplicates upon landing and explodes.

WINGMAN (Q)

EQUIP Wingman. FIRE to send Wingman forward to concuss the first enemy he sees. ALT FIRE to plant/defuse.

DIZZY (E)

EQUIP Dizzy. FIRE to send Dizzy soaring forward. Dizzy charges then unleashes plasma blasts at enemies.

THRASH (X)

EQUIP Thrash. FIRE to link with Thrash's mind and steer her through enemy territory, exploring to detain enemies.

KILLJOY

NANOSWARM (C)

EQUIP a Nanoswarm grenade. FIRE to throw. ACTIVATE to deploy a damaging swarm of nanobots.

ALARMBOT (Q)

EQUIP a covert Alarmbot. FIRE to deploy a bot that hunts down enemies that get in range, leaving them vulnerable.

TURRET (E)

EQUIP a Turret. FIRE to deploy a turret that fires at enemies in a 180 degree cone. HOLD EQUIP to recall.

LOCKDOWN (X)

EQUIP the Lockdown device. FIRE to deploy the device. After a long windup, the device detains all enemies caught in radius.

PHOENIX

BLAZE (C)

EQUIP a flame wall. FIRE to create a line of flame that blocks vision and damages players passing through.

CURVEBALL (Q)

EQUIP a flare orb. FIRE to throw it left or right, detonating and blinding any player who sees the orb.

HOT HANDS (E)

EQUIP a fireball. FIRE to throw a fireball that creates a lingering fire zone, damaging enemies and healing Phoenix.

RUN IT BACK (X)

INSTANTLY place a marker at Phoenix's location. Dying or time expiring brings Phoenix back with full health.
