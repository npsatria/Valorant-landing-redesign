CHOOSE YOUR AGENT

Creativity is your greatest weapon. More than guns and bullets, you'll choose an Agent armed with adaptive, swift, and lethal abilities that create opportunities to let your gunplay shine.

GEKKO

GEKKO

MOSH PIT (C)

EQUIP Mosh. FIRE to throw Mosh like a grenade. Mosh duplicates upon landing and explodes.

WINGMAN (Q)

EQUIP Wingman. FIRE to send Wingman forward to concuss the first enemy he sees. ALT FIRE to plant/defuse.

DIZZY (E)

EQUIP Dizzy. FIRE to send Dizzy soaring forward. Dizzy charges then unleashes plasma blasts at enemies.

THRASH (X)

EQUIP Thrash. FIRE to link with Thrash's mind and steer her through enemy territory.

KILLJOY

KILLJOY

ALARMBOT (Q)

EQUIP a covert Alarmbot. FIRE to deploy a bot that hunts down enemies.

TURRET (E)

EQUIP a Turret. FIRE to deploy a turret that fires at enemies in a 180 degree cone.

NANOSWARM (C)

EQUIP a Nanoswarm grenade. FIRE to throw and ACTIVATE to deploy a damaging swarm.

LOCKDOWN (X)

EQUIP the Lockdown device. FIRE to deploy the device to detain all enemies.

PHOENIX

PHOENIX

CURVEBALL (Q)

EQUIP a flare orb. FIRE to throw it left or right, blinding any player.

HOT HANDS (E)

EQUIP a fireball. FIRE to throw a fireball that damages enemies and heals Phoenix.

BLAZE (C)

EQUIP a flame wall. FIRE to create a line of flame that blocks vision and damages.

RUN IT BACK (X)

INSTANTLY place a marker. Dying or time expiring brings Phoenix back.
