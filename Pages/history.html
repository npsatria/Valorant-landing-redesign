THE VALORANT PROTOCOL

16 OCTOBER 2019

PROJECT "A"

Riot Games first hinted at a character-based tactical shooter codenamed "Project A" during the 10-year anniversary stream of League of Legends, promising lethal gunplay and unique abilities.

2 MARCH 2020

THE REVEAL

Project A is officially unveiled as VALORANT. First gameplay trailers are released, showcasing the hyper-natural cast of Agents and the fundamental tactical loop of the game.

2 JUNE 2020

IGNITION: GLOBAL LAUNCH

VALORANT enters full global release alongside the game's newest Agent, Reyna, and the launch map Ascent. A new era of tactical shooters officially begins.

26 JANUARY 2021

CHAMPIONS TOUR INAUGURATION

The VALORANT Champions Tour (VCT) is announced, establishing a year-long global esports circuit culminating in the first-ever global champion being crowned.
